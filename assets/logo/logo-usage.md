# Logo Usage

Official Mindscape logo guidelines from the 2022 Brand Manual Guideline.

## Logo Anatomy

The Mindscape logo consists of two parts:

- **Pictogram** — a line-art side profile of a face with a globe emerging from the head and sparkle stars, representing mental clarity and expanded thinking
- **Logotype** — the wordmark "MIND" (bold, spaced caps) stacked above "SCAPE" (light, spaced caps), using Myriad Variable Concept for English and GE SS Two for Arabic

## Clear Space (Isolation Area)

Keep a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the icon mark on all four sides. Nothing — text, imagery, or UI elements — should enter this zone.

## Placement Rules

- use the full logo (`logo-light.png` or `logo-dark.png`) on splash screens, onboarding, authentication screens, empty states, and brand-led surfaces
- use the icon-only variant (`app-icon.png`) where space is constrained: compact headers, in-app navigation badges, notification icons
- the full logo is the preferred form — use the icon alone only when necessary

## Background Rules

- on white or light neutral surfaces → `logo-light.png` (green logo)
- on the primary green (`#44aa87`) background → `logo-dark.png` (yellow logo)
- on navy (`#0d2347`) background → white or yellow variant (from source files)
- avoid placing any logo variant on busy photography, textured gradients, or low-contrast surfaces

## App Notes

- app icon: use `app-icon.png` as the base and keep the mark simple and readable at small sizes
- notification and status surfaces should use a simplified single-color mark when required by platform rules
- splash and launch surfaces should use `logo-dark.png` centered on the primary green background
- do not use the full wordmark logo as the app icon

## Logo Misuse

Do not:

- stretch or compress the logo in any direction
- rotate the logo
- recolor using any colors outside the approved brand palette
- add shadows, glows, gradients, or outlines to the mark
- separate the pictogram from the wordmark except in approved icon-only contexts
- use yellow logo on white backgrounds (contrast failure)
- recreate or redraw any part of the logo

## Wordmark Spacing

The wordmark follows the construction grid from the brand manual:
- "MIND" sits centered over "SCAPE"
- vertical spacing between icon and wordmark is fixed — do not adjust
- maintain the relative distance between font and icon at all times
